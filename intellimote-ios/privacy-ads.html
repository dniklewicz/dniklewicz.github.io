Privacy Policy

What data is sent to and processed by any external service?

Intellimote is a standalone, zero-configuration application and is not connecting to any external servers. All data collected by the Application (like UPnP ip addresses and basic metadata) which is needed by Intellimote to operate and provide access to application's features (exluding advertisments) is stored locally on the user's device.


Advertising in the app

Intellimote for iOS show advertisements. This might be banners, full screen ads or short videos and you can through an in-app purchase avoid advertisements to be shown.
The advertisements you see are provided by third party solution which is AdMob by GoogleÂ®.


Advertising Identifiers

Advertising identifiers are identifiers that devices, such as mobile phones, provide to identify that device to advertising partners. This identifier is used by advertisers to serve more relevant personalized advertisements in Intellimote. You can opt out of receiving personalized ads on your phone, by turning off the advertising identifier on your device. The exact procedures vary from device to device but typically you can change it in the privacy section of your device preferences. Please note that you will still see advertisements if you turn off the advertising identifier, but they will not be based on your profile and may therefore be less relevant to you.

Privacy Policy

What data is sent to and processed by any external service?

Intellimote is a standalone, zero-configuration application and is not collecting any kind of a private data. All data collected by the Application (like UPnP ip addresses and basic metadata) which is needed by Intellimote to operate and provide access to application's features is stored locally on the user's device.
